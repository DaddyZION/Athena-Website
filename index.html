Athena combines AI-powered personalization with project-based learning to solve the tripartite crisis in modern education: student disengagement, educator burnout, and the relevance gap.

Discover Athena See the benefits

  • The Tripartite Crisis

    Student Disengagement: One in four pupils in England disengage when moving to secondary school. Educator Burnout: Teachers are drowning in administrative tasks and marking. Relevance Gap: Students feel unprepared for their futures and the workforce demands.

  • How Athena Solves This

    Athena allows teachers and students to curate personalized custom coursework, mock exams, and tutorials. Using natural language, teachers specify topics and requirements, and our AI creates engaging lessons tailored to each student's interests—freeing teachers to focus on mentoring.

  • Key Advantages

    Centralized Data: All resources in one place. Enhanced Communication: Shared spaces for feedback and collaboration. Data-Driven Insights: Real-time monitoring of student performance for teachers, tutors, and parents.
